About Us

At Adaa Jewellery, we celebrate the timeless art of adornment. Jewellery is not just an accessory it is a reflection of grace, culture, and personality. Whether it's a sparkling diamond ring or a traditional gold necklace, each piece holds a story that is as unique as the person wearing it.

From ancient times to modern trends, jewellery has always been a symbol of love, prestige, and beauty. At Adaa, we craft every ornament with precision, passion, and a deep respect for tradition, blending it beautifully with contemporary elegance.

Discover the world of exquisite craftsmanship, rare gemstones, and artistic design only at Adaa Jewellery, where every jewel tells a story and every sparkle adds a little more magic to your life.


About Jewellary


Kundan Jewellary

Kundan jewellery is a traditional form of Indian jewellery known for
its intricate designs and use of precious and semi-precious stones set in gold.

Diamond Jewellary

Diamonds are the hardest gemstones in the world, belong to the modifications of carbon and are marked with the four C's.

Gold Jewellary

Gold jewelry is a popular choice due to its beauty, durability, and historical significance. Purity is measured in karats, with 24k being the purest (99.9%)

Silver Jewellary

Centuries of symbols surround silver jewellery. Starting with the ancient Indians, down to the indigenous people of South America

Platinum Jewellary

Platinum is an excellent value because of its inherent qualities: its purity, its strength, its durability and its rarity.
